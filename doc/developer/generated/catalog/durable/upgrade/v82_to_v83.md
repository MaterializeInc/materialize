# catalog::durable::upgrade::v82_to_v83

Migration from catalog version 82 to 83.
Repairs Role rows left in an inconsistent state by the v80->v81 migration's `auto_provision_source` backfill, which silently no-opped on non-cloud environments and left those Role rows in their pre-v81 byte form. When a subsequent DDL operation (`ALTER ROLE`, `DROP ROLE`, or role membership change) retracted such a row using the current proto (which includes the field explicitly), the retraction failed to consolidate, leaving dangling `-1` rows in the persist shard.

The migration scans the snapshot for Role rows matching the v80-form-drift signature — a dangling `-1` plus at least one parsed-equal stale `+1` sibling — and emits compensating `+1` and `-1` updates to cancel the phantom and retract the stale row. Rows that do not fit the structural signature (no parsed-equal sibling, ambiguous live state, unexpected diff magnitude, non-Role kinds) are logged at WARN and left for human review. `RepairStats` tracks counts of repaired, stale-retracted, and skipped rows.
