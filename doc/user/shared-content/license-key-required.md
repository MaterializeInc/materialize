- To get a license key for Materialize Community Edition, visit
  [the "License" page of your Materialize Console](https://console.materialize.com/license),
  or https://materialize.com/self-managed/community-license if you do not
  have a cloud account.

- To purchase a Materialize Self-Managed Enterprise License, contact us
  [here](https://materialize.com/self-managed).
