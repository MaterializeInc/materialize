- v26.16.0 introduces a new `v1alpha2` version of the Materialize CRD
  - This new CRD version removes the `requestRollout` field, and all previously deprecated fields.
  - When the Materialize CR is changed, a rollout will begin automatically.
  - The Materialize Operator now hosts conversion webhooks to automatically
    convert from `v1alpha1` to `v1alpha2`.
  - You may continue to apply `v1alpha1` CRs, but they will be converted to `v1alpha2`
    and the new rollout behavior will be used. To avoid confusion, it is recommended
    that you migrate to `v1alpha2` CRs at your earliest convenience.
