- v26.17.0 introduces a new `v1alpha2` version of the Materialize CRD
  - This new CRD version removes the `requestRollout` field, and all previously deprecated fields.
  - When the Materialize CR is changed using `v1alpha2`, a rollout will begin automatically.
  - The Materialize Operator now hosts conversion webhooks to automatically
    convert from `v1alpha1` to `v1alpha2`.
  - You may continue to apply `v1alpha1` CRs for now. We recommend you opt-in to `v1alpha2` at your convenience, as we will migrate to the `v1alpha2` behavior in the next major release.
